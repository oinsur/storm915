About Us

We are a newly established team from El Paso, Texas. Driven by a mission to empower girls and bring them back to the soccer field, our primary goal is to nurture their soccer skills and instill confidence, enabling them to inch closer towards their dreams of playing collegiate soccer.

Impact

On the field, we are dedicated to creating a supportive and development-focused environment. Our experienced coaching staff works tirelessly to enhance the technical abilities of our players, while also emphasizing teamwork, sportsmanship, and resilience. We believe that through the beautiful game of soccer, we can impart valuable life lessons that extend far beyond the pitch.

Giving Back

Our girls actively engage with elementary and middle school-aged girls, providing one-on-one mentorship and assisting them in developing their own soccer skills. This initiative not only benefits the mentees but also fosters leadership qualities and a sense of responsibility within our own team members.

In addition to skill development and community engagement, we also prioritize providing exposure to competitive outdoor and indoor settings both locally and regionally. Through participation in tournaments, leagues, and friendly matches, our team gains valuable experience and hones their abilities in various environments.

Furthermore, we firmly believe in cultivating financial responsibility and independence in our players. As part of our program, our girls actively participate in the fundraising process, learning essential skills related to financial management and resourcefulness. By involving them in this aspect, we aim to equip them with vital life skills that will serve them well beyond their soccer journey.

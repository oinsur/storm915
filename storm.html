About Us

We are a newly established team from El Paso, Texas. Driven by a mission to empower girls and bring them back to the soccer field, our primary goal is to nurture their soccer skills and instill confidence, enabling them to inch closer towards their dreams of playing collegiate soccer.

Impact

On the field, we are dedicated to creating a supportive and development-focused environment. Our experienced coaching staff works tirelessly to enhance the technical abilities of our players, while also emphasizing teamwork, sportsmanship, and resilience. We believe that through the beautiful game of soccer, we can impart valuable life lessons that extend far beyond the pitch.
